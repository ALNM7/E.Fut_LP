About Us

Welcome to Our Soccer Family

Champions Youth Soccer Academy is dedicated to providing young athletes with a comprehensive soccer education. We believe in developing not just skilled players, but well-rounded individuals who understand the values of sportsmanship, dedication, and respect.

Our Vision

Shaping Tomorrow's Leaders Through Soccer

To be the premier youth soccer academy recognized for developing exceptional athletes who excel both on and off the field, inspiring a lifelong love for the beautiful game.

We envision a community where every child has the opportunity to discover their potential, build lasting friendships, and grow into confident, responsible individuals through the transformative power of soccer.

Our Mission

Empowering Young Athletes to Reach Their Full Potential

Our mission is to provide high-quality soccer training in a supportive and inclusive environment, fostering technical excellence, physical fitness, and personal growth. We are committed to developing confident, disciplined, and compassionate individuals who embody the true spirit of the game.

Rules & Regulations

Academy Guidelines

Clear expectations for a safe, respectful, and productive environment

1. Attendance & Punctuality

  • Players must attend at least 75% of scheduled training sessions
  • Arrive 10 minutes before session start time
  • Notify coaches 24 hours in advance for absences
  • Repeated tardiness may result in reduced playing time

2. Conduct & Behavior

  • Respect coaches, teammates, opponents, and officials at all times
  • No bullying, discrimination, or inappropriate language
  • Follow all instructions from coaching staff immediately
  • Demonstrate good sportsmanship in victory and defeat

3. Dress Code & Equipment

  • Wear official academy uniform for all matches and events
  • Shin guards are mandatory for all training and matches
  • Appropriate soccer cleats must be worn (no metal studs)
  • Remove all jewelry before training and matches

4. Safety & Health

  • Players must be physically fit and cleared by a doctor
  • Report all injuries immediately to coaching staff
  • Stay hydrated and bring water to all sessions
  • No training or playing while sick or injured

5. Parent & Guardian Responsibilities

  • Ensure timely drop-off and pick-up of players
  • Communicate any medical conditions or concerns
  • Support positive behavior and sportsmanship
  • Respect coaching decisions and academy policies

6. Academy Policies

  • Zero-tolerance policy for violence or threats
  • Academy reserves the right to dismiss players for violations
  • All fees are non-refundable once the season begins
  • Photography/video for promotional use requires consent

Important Notice

By enrolling in Champions Youth Soccer Academy, parents/guardians and players agree to abide by all academy rules and regulations. Violations may result in suspension or dismissal from the program. We are committed to maintaining a safe, positive, and enriching environment for all participants.
